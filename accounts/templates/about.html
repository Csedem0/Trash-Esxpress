Who Are We

Trash Express is a platform owned by Hadsh Technologies that utilizes software applications and data to promote a circular economy and achieve sustainability through waste management. We focus on using technology to protect the environment and generate profit.

What We Do

We connect ecosystem stakeholder by ensuring ecosystem inclusion that connects waste generators to waste collectors across the value chain. Our innovation has created a waste-on-demand solution. Furthermore we provide a learning platform for waste generators to know how to handle waste and ensure a cleaner environment.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to achieve scarcity with digital tools and build a sustainable robust circular economy one community/state at a time.

Our Vision.

Our vision is to become a global waste technology services provider that meets all global waste challenges.
